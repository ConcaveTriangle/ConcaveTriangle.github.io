Publications & Research

AI-assisted Legal Education for Student Grouping Using Large Language Models

Large language models (LLMs) have demonstrated transformative potential in fields like education and law. This paper examines their role as assistive tools for legal education through a system called LawEducator, an AI assistant designed for legal learning. Built on a pre-trained LLM with domain-specific fine-tuning and instructional alignment, LawEducator answers legal queries and integrates a retriever to source knowledge from legal texts. The paper also explores its application in educational practices like student grouping and analyzes the benefits and limitations of LLMs in legal education. Read more
